## Version Beta.1.1 Contains:

### 1. Market Simulation

- Fully simulated stock market featuring 50 publicly traded holdings.
- Holdings organized into industry sectors.
- Each holding tracks:
Share Price
Market Capitalization
Volatility
Sector Affiliation
Growth Conditions
Analyst Price Targets
- Prices react dynamically to:
Company Fundamentals
Economic Conditions
Sector Sentiment
World Events
Market Cycles

### 2. Market Intelligence

- Added dynamic news feed with automatically generated financial news.
- News responds to:
Economic Changes
Company Performance
Sector Developments
Global Events
Multiple in-universe financial publications generate unique reports.
- Analyst Coverage
- Added analyst research and recommendations.
- Analysts can:
Upgrade Holdings
Downgrade Holdings
Raise Price Targets
Lower Price Targets
Initiate Coverage

### 3. Sector Tracking
- Added real-time sector sentiment monitoring.
- Displays industry confidence levels and outlook ratings.
- Portfolio Management
- Create and manage multiple investor accounts.
- Buy and sell holdings in real time.
- Tracks:
Cash Balance
Net Worth
Realized P/L
Unrealized P/L
- Portfolio performance history and holdings overview.
- Trading Automation
- Automated buy and sell rules.
- Limit-buy functionality.
- Take-profit targets.
- Custom price-triggered purchases.

### 4. World Events
- Added dynamic world events capable of impacting:
Individual Holdings
Entire Sectors
Economic Indicators
Market Sentiment
- Includes wars, embargoes, discoveries, disasters, festivals, and more.

### 5. Dungeon Master Controls
- Direct control over:
Economic Growth
Inflation
Interest Rates
Global Trade
Political Stability
Market Cycles
- Trigger custom events and market shocks.
- Modify company and sector performance in real time.

### 6. Persistence & Data
- Save and restore market states.
- Import and export campaigns via JSON.
- Full transaction audit ledger.
- Historical market and portfolio tracking.
